Are you tired of sitting through lengthy cutscenes while playing Jedi Fallen Order? Well, I’ve got some good news for you! In this article, I’ll be sharing an easier way to activate a cutscene glitch that allows you to skip cutscenes and speed up gameplay by 10 times! Whether you’re playing on PlayStation or PC with a keyboard and mouse or a PS5 or PS4 controller, this glitch will work for you. Unfortunately, it does not work with an Xbox controller. So, let’s dive into the step-by-step process of activating this glitch and enjoy a faster gaming experience!

Step 1: Load Up the Game#

First things first, let’s load up Jedi Fallen Order on your console or PC. Make sure that you have in-game audio functioning properly. Take a moment to familiarize yourself with the menu sounds and other audio cues before proceeding to the next step.

Step 2: Activating the Audio Glitch#

Now, let’s activate the audio glitch that allows us to skip cutscenes. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Quit out of the game if it is already running.
  2. Load up the game again.
  3. As the game loads, pay attention to your inputs and the audio fades in.
  4. Once the game has loaded, you’ll notice that there is no audio.

Congratulations! You have successfully performed the first part of the glitch. Keep in mind that this glitch will only work with certain cutscenes, which we’ll explore in the next steps.

Step 3: Repeat the Audio Glitch#

Since the audio glitch needs to be activated for specific cutscenes, we’ll need to repeat the process. Follow these steps to continue:

  1. Return to the home screen of your console or PC.
  2. Wait for the audio to come back.
  3. Navigate to the media gallery in the game.
  4. Find a cutscene that you want to skip and select it.
  5. As the cutscene starts, you’ll notice that the game freezes on the first frame.
  6. Pause the game and put your PS5 into rest mode.

Step 4: Resuming from Rest Mode#

Once you have put your PS5 into rest mode, follow these steps to resume and activate the cutscene glitch:

  1. Wake your PS5 from rest mode.
  2. You’ll notice that the cutscene glitch and the audio glitch are in effect.
  3. Resume playing the game and enjoy the fast-forwarded cutscenes.
  4. If you want to watch more cutscenes at lightning speed, you can continue activating the glitch for each cutscene you encounter.

Safeguarding Your Glitch#

To make it easier for you to activate the cutscene glitch in the future, it’s recommended to keep a save file specifically for this purpose. By doing so, you can avoid going through the loading screens and activate the glitch directly. This will save you time and effort while playing Jedi Fallen Order.

With this glitch, you’ll be able to breeze through cutscenes and experience a much faster gameplay. Enjoy the benefits of skipping cutscenes and focus more on the action-packed gameplay of Jedi Fallen Order. Say goodbye to the frustration of waiting and hello to an enhanced gaming experience!
