How to Copy Your Sensitivity to Any Game with Kovaak's Sensitivity Matcher

Discover the simple yet powerful tool for copying your sensitivity settings from one game to another

Are you a gamer who struggles with adjusting to new sensitivity settings when switching between games? Introducing Kovaak’s Sensitivity Matcher, a free and reliable app developed by Cal Vox Kovac, a trusted name in the gaming community. Gone are the days of manually adjusting your sensitivity for each game. With this app, you can easily copy your sensitivity and ensure a seamless experience across different games.

Step-by-Step Guide#

  1. Identify the Game Engine: Start by determining the game engine of the game you want to copy your sensitivity to. For example, if you’re playing Apex Legends on the Source engine and your sensitivity is 2.9, note down these details.

  2. Launch Kovaak’s Sensitivity Matcher: Open the app and navigate to the “Game Engine” section. Enter the game engine details, such as “Source,” and input your sensitivity value, in this case, 2.9.

  3. Set the Target Point: Choose a fixed point in the game where you want your camera to rotate 360 degrees. This will be the reference for matching the sensitivity.

  4. Copy Your Sensitivity: Press the “Alt” and “Backspace” keys simultaneously to initiate the sensitivity matching process. The app will automatically adjust the sensitivity until it matches your desired rotation.

  5. Test in the Game: Launch the game and move your camera to perform a complete 360-degree rotation. If the sensitivity feels accurate, congratulations! You have successfully copied your sensitivity. If not, proceed to the next steps.

  6. Fine-Tuning the Sensitivity: If the sensitivity feels too fast or too slow, return to Kovaak’s Sensitivity Matcher and make adjustments accordingly. Lower the sensitivity value if it’s too fast or increase it if it’s too slow. Repeat the process until you achieve your desired sensitivity.

  7. Unknown Game Engine: If you are unsure of the game engine or it’s a new game, follow these steps: Enter your current sensitivity value in the “Game Sensitivity” section of the app. Launch the game and select a fixed point to rotate 360 degrees. Press “Alt” and “Backspace” to allow the app to analyze the rotation distance automatically.

  8. Fine-Tuning in Unknown Game Engines: The app will provide a suggestion for adjusting the sensitivity. If the rotation was too far, press “Alt” and “-” to decrease the sensitivity. If it was too close, press “Alt” and “+” to increase it. Keep fine-tuning until you achieve the desired sensitivity.

Benefits of Kovaak’s Sensitivity Matcher#

  1. Time-Saving: Kovaak’s Sensitivity Matcher eliminates the need for manual adjustments, saving you valuable time. Within minutes, you can copy your sensitivity and jump straight into the game.

  2. Seamless Transitions: By using this app, you can ensure a consistent sensitivity experience across various games. Say goodbye to fumbling with new settings every time you switch games.

  3. Easy-to-Use Interface: The app’s user-friendly interface makes it accessible to both novice and experienced gamers. Its simple design allows for a hassle-free sensitivity copying process.

  4. Free and Reliable: Kovaak’s Sensitivity Matcher is developed by Cal Vox Kovac, a reputable name in the gaming community. Rest assured that the app is trustworthy and won’t compromise your gaming experience.

In conclusion, Kovaak’s Sensitivity Matcher is a must-have tool for gamers who value consistent sensitivity settings across different games. By following the easy step-by-step process outlined above, you can copy your sensitivity effortlessly. Say goodbye to the frustration of adjusting sensitivities manually and embrace a seamless gaming experience. Try out this app and let us know your feedback. Happy gaming!
